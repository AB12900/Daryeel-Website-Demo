About Daryeel Youth

Caring for the Community

Daryeel (meaning "Care" in Somali) is a street outreach effort in Minneapolis providing East African youth with both basic needs and human connection. Every Friday night, volunteers meet with people on the streets of Cedar-Riverside, sharing food, love, and the power of relationships. Our outreach is an all-volunteer effort, focused on helping at-risk youth, including those dealing with homelessness.

Our Services

Basic Needs

Providing food, clean socks, hygiene products, and first-aid supplies.

Outreach & Support

Connecting youth with human interaction and emotional support.

Resource Connections

Helping families and individuals access rehab and other resources.

Community Building

Fostering relationships and a sense of belonging in the community.
